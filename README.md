# PILIGRIM_FOOTFALL_PREDICTION

🌍 Pilgrim Footfall Prediction System
🔮 AI-Powered Crowd Forecasting | Team: THE INTEGRATORS
📌 Overview

Managing large crowds at pilgrimage sites is a major challenge, especially during festivals and holidays. Sudden surges in visitors can lead to overcrowding, traffic congestion, and safety risks.

👉 Our solution uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to predict daily footfall, enabling better planning, improved safety, and enhanced visitor experience.

🎯 Problem Statement

Pilgrimage sites face:

🚨 Unpredictable crowd surges
🚗 Traffic congestion
⏳ Long waiting times
⚠️ Safety and management issues

Traditional estimation methods are inaccurate and inefficient.

💡 Our Solution

We built an AI-based predictive system that forecasts future visitor counts using:

📊 Historical footfall data
🌦️ Weather conditions
🎉 Festivals and holidays
📅 Day-of-week patterns

The system helps authorities:

Allocate resources efficiently
Manage crowd flow
Improve safety measures
